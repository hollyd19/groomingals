Products

We use all-natural products which are great for your dog's skin and environmentally friendly! We also provide a less stressful environment for your fur baby, with no kennels and no other dogs.

Personal Attention

You will receive one-on-one time with certified canine image consultant to advise you on the best cut for your pup and your lifestyle.

Hygeine

Bathing and grooming on a regular basis can prevent periodontal disease and bad breath, reduce skin and paw disorders, eliminate painful fur matting, and reduce excess shedding.

Expectations of Owners

All clients must provide proof of current vaccinations, including rabies.

Pricing

Because no dogs are exactly alike, each pet is priced differently. Below are some things we consider when pricing:

  • What size and breed is your dog?
  • Does your dog require an all-over body cut or just trimming in specific areas?
  • Does your dog shed excessively?
  • Is your dog matted?
  • Does your dog have special needs? Are they arthritic, nervous, or have a challenging personality?
  • How much individual time does your dog need?

You can always give us a call for an estimate!

Scheduling

Call us for an appointment and we can work out a time that fits for your lifestyle. Variety of slots available.

All after-hours calls will be promptly returned on the following business day.
